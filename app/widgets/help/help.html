table-editor is a lightweight web application that enables smart CSV and TSV editing that is guided by a DOSDP - Dead Simple Ontology Design Patterns specified in YAML. table-editor provides autocomplete lookup and other assistance during the process of creating or editing portions of knowledge graphs in a tabular format. The constrained and assisted environment ensures that the resulting CSV or TSV data is compatible with subsequent integration into a larger knowledgebase or ontology. Source available at GitHub Source
